Camera: the "Camera" movement must be selected.
- Rotate the camera: Mouse left + Mouse movement.
- Move the camera: ASDWQE keys.
- Zoom: Scroll wheel.
- Place camera at the beginning: Press the button "Init position".
- Place camera at the left eye: Press the button "Left eye".
- Place camera at the right eye: Press the button "Right eye".

Scene: the "Scene" movement must be selected.
- Move the scene: ASDWQE keys.
- Scale the scene: Scroll wheel.

User eyes: the "Eyes" movement must be selected.
- Move the eyes: ASDWQE keys.
- Change interocular distance: Scroll wheel.

Load model: A model in ".obj" format can be loaded by
dragging and dropping the ".obj" and the ".mtl" (if exists)
at the same time over the "Drag & drop" box.
